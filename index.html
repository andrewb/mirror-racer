Mirror Racer

2022 JS1024 Entry – 1019 bytes

Mobile: Tap on the left and right of the canvas to move.

Desktop: Use Left Arrow and Right Arrow to move.

Make the white player win by leaving the blue opponent behind! Green cells will boost while red will slow you to a crawl. The blue player will mirror your moves!

Each course is unique. Some are harder than others, so try a few times.
